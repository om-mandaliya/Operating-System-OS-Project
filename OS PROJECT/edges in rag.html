Edges IN RAG

Screenshot of resourse allocation graph

  • Edges in rag also of two types,one represents assignment other represents the wait of a process for a resourse. the above image shows each of item.

  • A resourse is shown as assigned to a process if the tail of the arrow is attached to an instance to the resourse and the head is attached to a process.

  • A process is shown as waiting for a resourse if the tail of an arrow is attached to the process while the head is pouinting towards the resourse
